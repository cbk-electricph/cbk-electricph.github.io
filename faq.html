FAQ's

Cause: When the unit is in the idle state the water in the pump reaches max pressure causing the motor to switch off.

Solution: Engage the trigger of the gun to let water flow out and relieve the pressure in the pump

Cause: The extension cord is too long or is not the proper gauge.

Solution: Replace the extension cord with either a 25’ (7.6 m) 12/3-gauge cord or a 50’ (15 m) 10/3-gauge cord.

Cause: Electrical outlet does not supply adequate power.

Solution: Try different outlet.

Cause: Motor suddenly stopped during continuous running.

Solution: Motor protector has activated allow to cool for 5 to 10 minutes.

Cause: No water supply.

Solution: Connect to water supply or check if water source was interrupted.

Cause: Water filter clogged up.

Solution: Clean water filter.

Cause: Nozzle clogged up.

Solution: Remove clogged by using nozzle pin.

Cause: Diameter of garden hose is too small.

Solution: Replace with a ½ inch or 5/8 garden hose.

Cause: Water supply is restricted.

Solution: Check water hose for kinks, leaks and blockage.

Cause: Not enough inlet water supply.

Solution: Open water source at full level.

Cause: Water inlet filter is clogged.

Solution: Remove filter and rinse out with warm water.

Cause: Spray gun is on low pressure setting/level

Solution: Set the spray gun to high level setting.

Cause: Pump is sucking air.

Solution: Check that hoses and fittings are air tight, turn off machine and purge pump by squeezing trigger gun until a steady flow of water emerges through the nozzle.

Cause: Water inlet filter is clogged.

Solution: Remove filter and rinse out with warm water.

Cause: Discharge nozzle is obstructed

Solution: Blow out or remove debris with supplied nozzle needle.

Cause: Calcified gun, hose, or spray wand

Solution: Run distilled vinegar through snow foam lance.

Cause: Spray wand not properly attached.

Solution: Insert the spray wand into the trigger gun. Carefully press in against the spring tension and twist into the locked position.

Cause: Broken o-ring or plastic insert.

Solution: Please contact our customer support.

Cause: Loose connector fittings.

Solution: Tighten fittings.

Cause: Water seals are damaged or worn out.

Solution: Please contact our customer support.

Cause: Oil seals are damaged or worn out.

Solution: Please contact our customer support.
